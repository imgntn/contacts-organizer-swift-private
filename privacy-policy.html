Privacy Policy for Contacts Organizer

Last Updated: November 3, 2025

Privacy First: Contacts Organizer is built with privacy at its core. All contact processing happens locally on your Mac. Your contact data never leaves your device.

1. Information We DON'T Collect

Contacts Organizer does NOT collect, store, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your contact information (names, phone numbers, email addresses, etc.)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Any personally identifiable information
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data

2. How the App Works

Contacts Organizer operates entirely on your local Mac computer:

  • Local Processing: All analysis, duplicate detection, and data quality checks happen on your Mac.
  • No Cloud Services: We do not use any cloud services or remote servers.
  • No Network Connections: The app does not connect to the internet or transmit any data.
  • Direct Contacts Access: The app only accesses your contacts through Apple's official Contacts framework with your explicit permission.

3. Data Storage

All data created by Contacts Organizer is stored locally on your Mac:

  • Backups: When you create backups, they are saved to locations you choose on your Mac and in your local Application Support folder.
  • Settings: Your app preferences are stored in macOS UserDefaults on your device.
  • No External Storage: We never store or sync your data to external servers or cloud storage.

4. Permissions

Contacts Organizer requests the following permissions:

  • Contacts Access: Required to read, analyze, and organize your contacts. You can revoke this permission at any time in System Settings > Privacy & Security > Contacts.
  • AppleEvents (Automation): Used only to open specific contacts or groups in the Contacts app when you click "View" buttons. This does not provide access to any additional data.
  • File Access: Only when you explicitly choose to save or open backup files.

5. Third-Party Services

Contacts Organizer does not integrate with, share data with, or use any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking systems.

6. App Sandbox

Contacts Organizer operates within Apple's App Sandbox, which provides additional security and privacy protection by:

  • Limiting the app's access to only the resources it needs
  • Preventing unauthorized access to your files and data
  • Ensuring the app cannot access network resources

7. Data Security

Your contact data security is managed by:

  • Apple's Contacts framework security
  • macOS file system permissions
  • App Sandbox restrictions
  • No transmission over networks (because we don't connect to the internet)

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • All your contact data remains in your Apple Contacts database
  • You can revoke Contacts access permission at any time
  • Uninstalling the app removes all app-related data from your device
  • Backups you create are under your complete control

9. Children's Privacy

Contacts Organizer does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app does not collect data from any users.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Since we don't collect any data, changes will typically only clarify our privacy practices.

11. Compliance

Contacts Organizer complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • macOS App Sandbox requirements
  • Privacy best practices for macOS applications
