Contacts Organizer Support

Welcome to Contacts Organizer support. Find answers to common questions and learn how to get the most out of the app.

Getting Started

Key Features:

  • Duplicate Detection: Automatically finds duplicate contacts using smart matching
  • Merge Contacts: Combine duplicates while preserving all information
  • Data Quality: Identify contacts with missing or incomplete information
  • Smart Groups: Automatically organize contacts by organization, criteria, or custom rules
  • Contact Backup: Create safe backups of your contacts before making changes

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my contact data safe?

Yes! All processing happens locally on your Mac. Your contact data never leaves your device. We recommend creating a backup before making any changes.

Q: How do I grant Contacts permission?

When you first launch the app, you'll be prompted to grant Contacts access. If you denied permission, you can enable it in:

System Settings → Privacy & Security → Contacts

Then check the box next to "Contacts Organizer".

Q: How do I create a backup?

You can create a backup in two ways:

  • First Launch: You'll be prompted to create a backup on first launch
  • Settings: Open Settings (⌘,) → General → Click "Backup All Contacts"

Contacts Organizer creates TWO backups: one in your chosen location and a safety copy in the app's folder.

Q: How does duplicate detection work?

The app uses multiple matching strategies:

  • Exact name matching
  • Similar name matching (fuzzy matching)
  • Same phone number
  • Same email address

Each duplicate group shows a confidence score to help you decide which matches are accurate.

Q: What happens when I merge contacts?

When merging contacts:

  • All phone numbers, emails, and addresses are combined (no duplicates)
  • Organization info, notes, and photos are preserved
  • The duplicate contacts are deleted
  • You choose which contact becomes the "primary" one to keep

Important: This action cannot be undone, so create a backup first!

Q: What are Smart Groups?

Smart Groups automatically organize your contacts based on:

  • Organization: Groups contacts by company/organization
  • Custom Rules: Create groups based on criteria like "has photo", "missing email", etc.

Smart groups are read-only and help you visualize your contact organization.

Q: Can I undo changes?

Contact merges and deletions cannot be undone within the app. However, you can restore from your backup:

  1. Open the Contacts app
  2. Go to File → Import
  3. Select your backup .vcf file

This is why we strongly recommend creating a backup before making changes!

Q: Why can't the app access my contacts?

If you're having permission issues:

  1. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security → Contacts
  2. Ensure "Contacts Organizer" is checked
  3. If it's not in the list, try restarting the app
  4. If problems persist, try restarting your Mac
Q: How do I reset the onboarding?

To see the onboarding tutorial again:

  1. Open Settings (⌘,)
  2. Go to the General tab
  3. Scroll to Debug section
  4. Click "Reset Onboarding"

Tips & Best Practices

  • Always Backup First: Create a backup before merging or cleaning up contacts
  • Review Duplicates: Check the confidence score and review matches before merging
  • Use Smart Groups: Identify contacts missing important information
  • Regular Cleanup: Periodically check for duplicates and data quality issues
  • Auto-Refresh: Enable auto-refresh in Settings for the latest contact data

System Requirements

  • macOS 14.0 or later
  • Contacts access permission
